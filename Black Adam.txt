Black Adam (Teth/Theo-Adam) is a supervillain and antihero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. 
Created by Otto Binder and C. C. Beck, the character is one of the archenemies of the superhero Captain Marvel 
(aka Shazam) and the nemesis of the Marvel Family (aka Shazam Family).
Cast. Dwayne Johnson as Teth-Adam / Black Adam: An antihero from Kahndaq who was imprisoned for 5,000 years. He will 
become the arch-rival of the superhero Shazam and shares his powers from the ancient wizard of the same name.
